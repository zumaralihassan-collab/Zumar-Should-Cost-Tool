## **Step 2: Choose Your Path**

### **Path A: "I need a should-cost estimate"**
1. Click the **Should Cost Calculator** tab (default)
2. Enter your **product name** (e.g., "custom injection-molded bracket")
3. Select **category** (auto-detect or pick one: construction, cosmetics, electronics, etc.)
4. Enter **order quantity**
5. Pick **region** (India, China, Vietnam, USA, EU, etc.)
6. Click **"Analyze with AI"**
7. AI auto-populates cost drivers → Edit with your market data
8. Live chart updates as you change parameters
9. **Export to Excel** for your negotiation room

**Time to result:** 3–5 minutes

---

### **Path B: "I need to run Kraljic on my spend"**
1. Click the **Supply Chain Analytics** tab
2. Scroll to **Kraljic Portfolio Matrix**
3. Review the 4-quadrant strategy (Strategic / Bottleneck / Leverage / Routine)
4. See example items in each quadrant
5. Plan your sourcing strategy per quadrant

**Time to insight:** 2 minutes

---

### **Path C: "I want to optimize my inventory"**
1. Click the **Supply Chain Analytics** tab
2. Scroll to **EOQ & Safety Stock Optimizer**
3. Enter: Annual demand, order cost, holding cost, lead time, service level
4. See **EOQ** (order quantity), **Safety Stock**, **Reorder Point** calculated
5. Try different scenarios with the sliders

**Time to optimization:** 5 minutes

## **Common Questions**

### Q: Is my data stored on a server?
**A:** No. Everything runs in your browser. No data leaves your machine. 100% private.

### Q: Can I use this offline?
**A:** The initial load needs internet (CDN for Tailwind, Chart.js), but once loaded, most features work offline except the AI chatbot.

### Q: Can I import my own data?
**A:** Currently, the app has built-in templates + sample data. You can manually edit all parameters.

### Q: What if my product isn't in the templates?
**A:** Pick "Generic" template or choose a similar category. All lines are editable — customize everything.

### Q: How accurate is the should-cost?
**A:** As accurate as your input data. The app builds cost *from first principles* (materials, labor, energy, overhead). Compare your numbers to market intelligence, RFQs, or supplier quotes.

### Q: Can I save my analysis?
**A:** Yes. The **"Save Scenario"** button stores up to 10 scenarios in your browser's storage. Comparisons are in-browser. To keep permanently, export to Excel.

### Q: How do I share my analysis?
**A:** Export to Excel (via app) or Print to PDF. Send the file to stakeholders.

## **Real-World Use Cases**

### **Use Case 1: Pre-Negotiation Anchor**
- Supplier quotes $4.50/unit
- You build should-cost: $3.85/unit with 12% margin
- You anchor at $4.28/unit (should-cost + 11.2%)
- **Typical outcome:** Supplier concedes to $4.15 (vs. initial $4.50)
- **Savings:** $0.35/unit × 100K units = **$35K**

### **Use Case 2: Redirect High-Cost Spending**
- You run Kraljic on your $50M spend
- Find that "Strategic" quadrant is 35% of spend (typical: 15–20%)
- You consolidate 5 Strategic suppliers to 2 via dual-sourcing
- **Typical outcome:** 8–12% cost reduction on Strategic items
- **Savings:** $50M × 0.35 × 0.10 = **$1.75M**

### **Use Case 3: Inventory Optimization**
- Your EOQ analysis shows: Order 2,000 units every 14 days (vs. current 3,500 every 21 days)
- You reduce average inventory from 5,250 units to 3,500 units
- Holding cost was $2.50/unit/year
- **Savings:** 1,750 units × $2.50 = **$4,375/year**

### **Use Case 4: Tariff Shock Response**
- New 25% tariff on Chinese imports hits
- Your annual import is $5M; elasticity is -1.3
- Simulator shows: Direct cost +$1.25M, volume drops 32.5%
- You immediately shift 40% of orders to Vietnam sourcing
- **Outcome:** Mitigate net impact to +$400K vs. worst-case $1.25M

## **Pro Tips**

✅ **Use ABC analysis to prioritize** — Spend time on the 20% that drives 80% of cost

✅ **Index-link pricing for Strategic items** — Kills supplier speculation on raw materials
