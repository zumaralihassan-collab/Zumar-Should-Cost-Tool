How Should Cost AI works

  1. Describe what you're sourcing. Name + a one-liner is enough. You don't need the engineering BoM — the AI engine will fill the gaps.
  2. AI analyzes the product and proposes every relevant cost driver — raw materials, labor, energy, equipment, facility, logistics, overhead, margin — based on a built-in procurement knowledge base spanning 12+ industries.
  3. Tune every parameter. Edit quantities, rates and yields. Sliders let you stress-test ±30% on materials / labor / energy.
  4. Get a should-cost per unit and for the whole order, with a clean breakdown chart. Compare against supplier quotes to negotiate from a position of evidence.
  5. Save scenarios for side-by-side comparison (e.g. China vs India vs Vietnam) and export everything to Excel for the sourcing file.

Built for sourcing managers, GSM teams, category leads, and cost engineers. All rates are editable defaults — replace with verified market data for production decisions.
