Demand Forecasting Engine

Exponential Smoothing + AR(1) models with MAPE/RMSE accuracy metrics — based on my Toyota SKU forecasting project

1

Input Historical Demand

Paste at least 12 historical periods (months/weeks). Tool auto-detects trend, seasonality, and generates forecast with confidence bands.

📚 About these models

Simple Exponential Smoothing (SES) — Flat-trend, fast-adapting; α weights recent observations more heavily.

Holt's Linear Trend — Adds a trend component; great when demand is steadily growing or declining.

Holt-Winters Triple Exp — Adds seasonality; ideal for SKUs with quarterly or annual cycles.

AR(1) ARIMA — Autoregressive component captures momentum; the workhorse for stable-demand items.

In my Toyota SKU project I combined these models with ChatGPT-assisted demand sensing and reduced forecast error by 15% — see About the Creator.
