# 📊 Should Cost AI — Procurement & Supply Chain Intelligence Suite

**🚀 Live Demo:** https://zumaralihassan-collab.github.io/Zumar-Should-Cost-Tool/

An **AI-powered procurement should-cost calculator** with executive dashboards, supply chain analytics, AI strategist chatbot, and auto-generated negotiation playbooks — built for procurement professionals, supply chain leaders, and category managers.

---

## 🎯 What It Does

Powered by **27+ procurement frameworks** and real-world sourcing experience:

### 1️⃣ **Should Cost Calculator**
- AI-powered product analyzer that **auto-detects cost drivers**
- Raw materials, packaging, labor, energy, equipment, facility overhead, QC, logistics, margin
- Sensitivity analysis (what-if sliders)
- Scenario comparison and Excel export
- Built-in templates: brick, perfume, apparel, PCBA, bottles + generic skeleton

### 2️⃣ **Executive Dashboard**
- KPI cards (total spend, realized savings, supplier OTIF, cost variance)
- Pareto spend by category (80/20 analysis)
- Regional sourcing breakdown
- Quarterly savings tracker
- Raw material cost indices (steel, plastic, cotton)
- **Top supplier scorecard** (40% quality, 30% OTIF, 20% cost, 10% sustainability)
- Risk heatmap (probability × impact matrix)

### 3️⃣ **Supply Chain Analytics**
- **Kraljic Portfolio Matrix** — Strategic / Bottleneck / Leverage / Routine sourcing strategies
- **ABC Analysis** — Pareto 80/20 SKU segmentation
- **Geographic footprint** — De-risk single-country dependency
- **EOQ & Safety Stock Optimizer** — Inventory optimization with service-level tuning
- **Lead time distribution** — Identify suppliers with high variability
- **Tariff impact simulator** — Quantify geopolitical sourcing shocks (regression-based)

### 4️⃣ **AI Procurement Strategist** (ChatBot)
Ask anything about:
- Kraljic Matrix, EOQ, RFQ/RFP/RFI, TCO, should-cost analysis
- Negotiation tactics (anchoring, bundling, indexing, BATNA)
- Supplier risk management, spend analysis, ABC segmentation
- Forecasting (ARIMA, exponential smoothing)
- Tariff mitigation, SRM, scorecards, Lean/Six Sigma, contracts, ESG

**Interactive chart rendering** — request pie, line, bar, scatter, pareto charts on demand.

### 5️⃣ **Strategic Insights & Playbook**
Auto-generated recommendations:
- Kraljic quadrant positioning
- Top cost driver focus
- Negotiation anchor and walk-away price
- Risk de-risking roadmap
- 6-step negotiation playbook
