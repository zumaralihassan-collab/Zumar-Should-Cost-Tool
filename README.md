## What's inside

A single-file web app with 5 integrated modules:

1. **Should Cost Calculator** — AI product analyzer proposes every cost driver (raw materials, packaging, labor, energy, equipment, facility, overhead, QC, logistics, margin). Sensitivity sliders, scenario comparison, Excel export.
2. **Executive Dashboard** — KPI cards, spend Pareto, regional doughnut, quarterly savings, raw material cost trends, supplier scorecards, risk heatmap.
3. **Supply Chain Analytics** — Interactive Kraljic Matrix, ABC analysis, geographic sourcing footprint, EOQ & Safety Stock optimizer, Tariff Impact Simulator.
4. **AI Procurement Strategist** — Chatbot trained on 27 procurement frameworks (Kraljic, EOQ, RFQ/RFP/RFI, TCO, negotiation, supplier risk, forecasting, tariffs, SRM, Lean/Six Sigma, contracts and more). Renders pie, line, bar, scatter, pareto charts on request.
5. **Insights & Recommendations** — Auto-generates strategic playbook from your should-cost analysis including a 6-step negotiation plan.
